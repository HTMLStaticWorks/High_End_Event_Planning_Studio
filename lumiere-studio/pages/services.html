Expertise & Offering

Comprehensive planning, design, and production tailored to your vision

We offer a limited number of full-service planning and design commissions annually. This ensures that every client receives our undivided attention, creativity, and commitment to perfection.

01.

Full Planning & Design

Our most comprehensive offering. We act as your project managers, financial advisors, creative directors, and confidants from the moment you say "yes" until the final guest departs.

  • Venue sourcing and site visits
  • Complete financial modeling and tracking
  • Curated vendor dream team assembly
  • Comprehensive event design and styling
  • Flawless day-of orchestration
02.

Event Styling & Floral Design

For clients who have secured their logistics but require an expert aesthetic touch. We conceptualize the visual narrative of your event, producing bespoke floral installations, tabletop curations, and atmospheric lighting.

  • Mood boards and color theory
  • Custom floral production
  • Textile, linen, and furniture sourcing
  • Custom stationery direction
03.

Destination Events

Whether on the shores of Lake Como or the countryside of Provence, we are experts in navigating the nuances of international event production bridging language barriers, cultural differences, and complex logistics.

  • Multi-day itinerary planning
  • Guest concierge and travel logistics
  • International vendor coordination
  • Cultural integration
