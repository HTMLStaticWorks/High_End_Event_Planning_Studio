Our Genesis

Born from a love of cinematic beauty

Solenza was founded on the belief that milestones deserve to be immortalized through exceptional design and flawless orchestration. What began as a boutique floral studio has organically evolved into a globally recognized event production firm.

We are a team of meticulous planners, conceptual designers, and logistical experts united by one goal: bringing the extraordinary to life.

"We don't follow trends; we observe the couple, the venue, and the season, letting authentic beauty reveal itself."
Creative Director

Eleanor Vance

With a background in architectural design and high-fashion editorial, Eleanor brings a structural yet wildly romantic sensibility to event styling. Her work is characterized by thoughtful restraint and a masterful use of space and light.
